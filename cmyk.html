Games of Berkeley

On November 2016, I participated at the CMYK Designation Jacob’s Institute for Design Innovation at UC Berkeley, an annual one-day design competition hosted by the student organization Innovation Design and sponsored by the Games of Berkeley.

Of the three prompts, my team (Melissa Silvers and Andre Mangulabnan) and I designed marketing material with an emphasis on the Games of Berkeley’s “Play Everywhere” slogan.  Over the course of seven hours, we produced two deliverables: an innovative flyer and a chessboard recyclable tote. Ultimately, our team’s submission placed 3rd and was named “Most Original,” by a judging panel of distinguished product designers and UI/UX designers.
