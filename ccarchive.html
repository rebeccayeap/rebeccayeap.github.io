Creative Commons Archive

product design

Creative Commons is a nonprofit organization that works to increase “the commons”, the body of work that is available to the public for free and legal sharing, and use. In January 2018, Creative Commons kicked off an new initiative called CC Usability and found many important findings, one being the need for an archiving process.

In November 2018, I began to work with CC's Director of Product and Research, Jane Park, on the need for archiving. Archiving allows creators to have control over their content without the fear of losing their work when a platform goes under. My work included included prototyping the archiving system for both mobile and web.
