Circle

a mobile app for students to browse, search, and propose student organizations

UC Berkeley, much like many large universities, is plagued with inefficiencies in the club recruitment and creation process. Just this past year, over 9000 fresh faces flooded onto campus to adjust to college life and find a new community away from home. With limited resources and staff, the various student services departments can only offer so much before students are inevitably left to fend for themselves, bombarded with hundreds of student organizations aggressively recruiting in a short time span. This cycle happens every semester at every college. With a hefty course load and hectic schedule, the last thing that should be an added burden are extracurriculars.
