CLYNX

Clynx is a health tech product which brings a gamified, home-based approach to physical therapy recovery. In July 2018, the startup was founded in Lisbon, Portugal by a group of biomedical and software engineers (Joana Pinto, Henrique Carvalho, Gonçalo Chambel, João Ramiro) and I. During our early stages, I worked extensively on market research and understanding the business objectives before designing two main interfaces: the web portal and the gamified exercises.

The process included a series of wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, meetings for improvement with physicians, and implementation with the software developers.
