IDEO CoLab

fall makeathon 2018

This past October, I had the opportunity to take part in IDEO CoLab's Makeathon and work on prototyping in emerging technologies under 6 hours. My team (Emily Deng, Hillary Hermawan, Santana Galgalikar) and I were paired together to find a way to triage emergency responses. We ultimately built a mobile application which uses real-time data and computer vision to match resources from donors to victims of natural disaster.

After reflecting on our experience, I wanted to challenge myself to build upon existing ideas and flush out two new features to test, so I spent a few days afterwards rethinking the problem to inform a redesign of our prototype. With even more time, I would want to perform user testing to guage the desirability, the feasibility for implementation, and the viability of our potential solution.
