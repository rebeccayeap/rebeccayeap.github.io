Hi. I’m Rebecca. As a junior pursuing degrees in business and product design at UC Berkeley, I’ve always been interested in how the intersection of both inform and rely on one another to afford a more creative, user-based approach. Through a range of experiences, I want to marry my passion for business, design, and tech.

I’ve always had an urge to create — 3D printed lightning earrings or coding this site — because the ability to transform my abstract ideas into real world solutions is what ultimately gratifies me. Otherwise, on any given day you can find me perched on a roof at sunset listening to experimental R&B (occasionally asmr) and fantasizing over medium-rare steak.
