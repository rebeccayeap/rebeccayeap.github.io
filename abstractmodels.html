abstract models

architecture | studio project

Alongside drawings are another medium, physical models, which have allowed me to explore the conventions of architectural representation. In Spring 2018, I iterated on a series of models that grappled with the conceptual possibilities and creative frictions inherent to translating different forms of representation.

The last project given in my ARCH 11B studio course focused on design based on a concept. I was given the words "merged" and "dense" to create a concept that was supported throughout my building design. Using the various pieces from the previous Siza House massing model, I rearranged them in a way that conveyed my concept. I initially only focused on tight, compact spaces but as I continued to create various iterations of my project, I redefined the words.
