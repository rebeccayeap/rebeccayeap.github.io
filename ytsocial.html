Google

UX DESIGN・13-WEEK TIMELINE・JUNE 2019

This past summer I was a UX design intern for Google on YouTube’s Social Distribution team. The Social Distribution made up of designers, researchers, engineers, and product managers work with different teams to enable creators and viewers to support one another.

As an intern, I researched, designed, and prototyped a more valuable experience for established creators to support aspiring creators, and viewers to support creators. During my summer at Google I also worked on two other side projects, designed the YouTube gear for 80+ interns, and worked with the program manager of my larger team on a quarterly newsletter.
